Support cards should not be something a hero can't do.
    - Supplies dropped
    - Ranged strikes
    - Travel
    - buffs
    - communication

Field Drop: $-(B)(B) Can only be used in Base. Gain 3 Potions of Healing.

Flame Strike: $-(a)(a)(a)(a) Deal 50 damage to all characters in a 4x4 space area within 6 spaces. Killed characters do not grant experience.

Teleport: $-(a)(a)(a)(a) Move to any friendly space.

Reality Shift: $- Reaction. Teleport to any space within 4 spaces.

Astral Sickness: $- Choose a character within 4 spaces. The target's speed is reduced by half until the end of its next turn.
